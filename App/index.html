Vegan cupcake app

Goals and risks

    Business goals

  • Sale more than 150 vegan cupcakes per week with new on-demand software
  • Partner with existing delivery service (electric vehicles/vans only) and use them to deliver vegan cupcakes
  • Make additional revenue by charging a delivery fee for every cupcake package order that is delivered

    Product goals

  • Get a user to sign-up
  • Allow users to select at least 6 cupcakes from the gallery
  • Allow users to select number of cupcakes
  • Allow users to enter address, phone number and payment details
  • Send a delivery information messages and emails to a user

    Users and their goals

  • Vegan cupcakes lovers within Amsterdam region
  • Users who want to have vegan cupcakes delivered to their home/office/celebration location

    The risks

  • Cupcakes are melting and not staying fresh upon delivery
  • Some ingredients might cause allergies
  • Too many refunds and we start to losing money due to the cost of delivery
